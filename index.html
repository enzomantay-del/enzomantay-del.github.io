About me

Where tourism meets technology

I currently serve as Municipal Tourism Director of Jardín América, Misiones, Argentina — a role I approach not just as a public administrator, but as someone who believes that technology is the most powerful tool for transforming how destinations are managed and experienced.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Tourism and a Diploma in Smart Tourism Destination Management from UNaM. At some point, I realized the digital tools I needed didn't exist — so I built them myself.

Today I develop full stack web applications with a focus on real-world impact in the tourism sector. My flagship project, TuriGest JA, is a municipal tourism management system currently live and in use in Jardín América.

I'm open to remote freelance projects at the intersection of tourism and web development.
